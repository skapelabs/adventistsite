About Us

Our Story

The roots of Adventist Health India trace back to a humble beginning, when a small group of dedicated medical missionaries arrived in India with a vision to provide compassionate care. This vintage photograph captures the spirit of our founding family, whose commitment to wholistic healing established the foundation of what would become a network of healthcare institutions across the country. Their legacy of faith-based service continues to inspire our mission today as we blend modern medical excellence with timeless values of integrity and compassion.

Our Mission

To establish, operate, and support institutions providing wholistic healthcare with compassion and integrity.

We are committed to serving communities across India with excellence in medical care while upholding our core values and faith-based principles.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of quality healthcare committed to healthy living and education.

We envision a future where our healthcare network continues to grow, bringing healing and hope to more communities throughout India.
