About Us

The Medical Trust of the Seventh-day Adventists (Adventist Health India) operates healthcare institutions nationwide with a mission to provide holistic care inspired by compassion, integrity, and faith.

Our healthcare network spans across India, bringing quality medical services to diverse communities while upholding our core values of excellence and ethical practice.
