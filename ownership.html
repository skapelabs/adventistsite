Ownership & Management

Learn about the organization behind Adventist Health India

The Medical Trust of the Seventh-day Adventists

The Medical Trust is a public charitable trust (Reg. No. 515 of 1999), owned and operated by the General Conference of Seventh-day Adventists, Southern Asia Division.

The Trust operates healthcare institutions across India with a mission to provide holistic care inspired by compassion, integrity, and faith.

Our healthcare institutions are committed to the highest standards of medical practice and ethical conduct, serving communities with excellence and respect for all individuals.

The Trust is governed by a Board of Trustees who oversee the operations and strategic direction of all healthcare facilities under its management.

Governance Structure

Board of Trustees

The Board of Trustees provides strategic direction and oversight for all Trust activities, ensuring alignment with our mission and values.

Executive Committee

The Executive Committee manages day-to-day operations and implements the strategic decisions made by the Board of Trustees.

Hospital Administration

Each hospital is managed by a dedicated administrative team responsible for local operations and community engagement.

Legal Status

The Medical Trust of the Seventh-day Adventists is registered as a public charitable trust under the Indian Trusts Act, with registration number 515 of 1999.

All our healthcare institutions operate in compliance with relevant healthcare regulations and standards set by national and state authorities.

The Trust is exempt from income tax under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, allowing us to reinvest all proceeds into improving healthcare services.
